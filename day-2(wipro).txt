Design empathy map, provide pain and gain points for a Parent persona admitting his/her child to a School in first class.

PARENT PERSONA WHILE JOINING THEIR CHILD IN SCHOOL

EMPATHY MAP

1.WHAT PARENT SAYS?
=>I WANT THE BEST SCHOOL?
=>WHERE CHILDREN CAN LEARN PRACTICALLY?
=>WHICH MAKES THEIR FOUNDATION STRONG?
=>WHICH ALLOWS ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IN STUDIES AND SPORTS FOR PHYSICAL HEALTH AND MENTAL HEALTH?
=>WHERE TEACHERS TREATS STUDENTS FRIENDLY AND PATIENTLY? 
=>WHICH SCHOOL GIVES PREFERENCE TO HYGENE?

2.WHAT A PARENT DOES?
=>PREPARE THE LIST OF SCHOOLS
=>COMPARE EACH SCHOOL WITH ANOTHER
=>ASK OTHERS TO KNOW MORE
=>AND LIST PROS AND CONS

3.WHAT PARENT THINKS?
=>I HAVE TO GIVE CHIDREN BEST SCHOOL
=>I HAVE TO DO SOME MORE WORK
=>THERE ARE SO MANY CHOICES 
=>WHY IT IS SO HARD TO MAKE DECISION
=>AM I THINKING TOO MUCH

4.HOW IT FEELS?
=>IT IS BIG RESPONSIBILITY
=>WHAT IF I MAKE WRONG CHOICE
=>IS IT GOOD THAT IAM PLANING TOO MUCH FOR 1ST CLASS
=>IT MAKES ME ANXIOUS ABOUT CHILD SAFETY

PAIN POINTS
===========
=>IT IS NOT ADJUSTABLE FOR HIM
=>WILL HE/SHE ABLE TO ADAPTE THE SCHOOL CULTURE
=>IT IS TOO HARD FOR HIM TO TRAVEL
=> HE/SHE  WILL BE TO SAFE OR NOT WITHOUT ME 
=>FOR A FIRST CLASS IT COSTS A LOT

GAIN POINTS
===========
=>WITH PROPER EDUCATION HE/SHE WILL ACHIEVE GOOD THINGS
=>HE/SHE WILL GET GOOD GRADES
=>THEY GET TO KNOW MORE PEOPLE & FRIENDS
